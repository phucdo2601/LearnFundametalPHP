Class - Object:
    - Class dung de dinh nghia chung 1 thuc the (Hieu don gian la mot ban thiet ke)
    - Object: thuc the la mo hinh hos viet trien khai cua mot lop thiet ke
    - Trong class se co thuoc tinh(bien), phuong thuc (ham), hang so (khai bao bang tu khoa const) 
    - Hieu thuc te hon: Class chinh la dong goi: bien ham hang so de su dung chat che hon

    - Tu khoa $this dung de truy cap cac phuong thuc, cac bien trong , mot class
    - Muon su dung mot hang so cua mot class trong php, thi chi can goi ten ClassName::NAME_CONSTANT

    + Kiem tra thuoc tinh ton tai:
        - C1: Kien tra bang ham isset() hoac empty()
        - C2: Kien tra thuoc tinh bang ham property_exists()

    + Kiem tra phuong thuc ton tai:
        - C1: dung ham method_exists(name_instance_obj, 'name_func')
